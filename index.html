Food Inspection Data Analysis and Modeling


Most health departments in the United States keep the record of food inspections for establishments on public record in some form, where all restaurant's prior violations are documented as well as all pertinent info regarding the what type of inspection occurred and how it resulted. But this information is stored in excel sheets or complex formats which are not easily interpretable and have virtually zero searchability. This project's goal is to visualise the the inspection track record of an establishment to potential customers in a captivating, easy to digest manner so they can make a better informed decision on where to eat.

  • Developed a web app enabling users to search restaurants, view inspection results, and analyze food safety trends.
  • Built a logistic regression model to predict inspection outcomes, helping identify high-risk establishments.
  • Engineered new features for the data set leveraging my domain knowledge.
